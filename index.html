Building Resilience Through Diversity

At EponaX, we value the resilience of nature that arises from its diversity, and our mission is to support the re-establishment of resilient ecosystems by letting nature lead through scientifically-backed strategies.

We specialize in using ecosystem engineers like the Exmoor pony—a rare breed native to Europe that we've dedicated decades to preserving—not just to protect their genetic legacy, but to harness their unique grazing patterns to promote biodiversity. Our goal is simple: to help you achieve a thriving, resilient ecosystem by working with nature. Whether it's rewilding your land, providing Exmoor ponies for your project, or offering ongoing genetic and ecological monitoring, we're here to make sure your vision for the future is sustainable and vibrant.

Our Services

  • Consultancy in rewilding and ecosystem resilience
  • Supplying Exmoor ponies for ecological recovery projects
  • Genetic and ecological monitoring of rewilding projects

Achmore

Achmore is home to our herd of Exmoor ponies, who serve as ecosystem engineers. Through their natural grazing patterns, they help improve biodiversity and promote the health of the land. Our work at Achmore is a living example of our commitment to ecological recovery and genetic conservation.

Research

  • Climate: Studies on the role of grazing animals in climate resilience.
  • Genetic Conservation: Preservation of the genetic diversity of rare breeds, with a focus on Exmoor ponies.
